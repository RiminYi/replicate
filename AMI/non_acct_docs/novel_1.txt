The afternoon sun cast long shadows across the cobblestone square. A gentle breeze rustled the leaves of the plane trees, offering a brief respite from the summer heat. Children chased pigeons near the central fountain, their laughter echoing off the ancient stone buildings. From a small cafe, the aroma of freshly brewed coffee and pastries drifted into the street, a welcoming invitation. He checked his watch, a simple silver timepiece, and realized he was early. He found an empty iron bench under a large tree and decided to wait, watching the city live its life around him. The journey had been long, but moments like this made it all worthwhile.
